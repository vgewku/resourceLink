Frequently Asked Questions

Is ResourceLink free to use for all users?
Yes! ResourceLink is completely free for users searching for community resources.
How do I save a resource?
When searching for resources, click "Save Search" to bookmark your preferred results.
How often is the information updated?
We update information regularly in collaboration with our community partners.
Can I suggest a new organization?
Absolutely! Please reach out via the Contact page to suggest a new resource.
How do I contact ResourceLink?
You can navigate to the Contact Us section on the top and submit your questions.
Do I need to create an account to use ResourceLink?
Yes both clients and provider have to create an account to join ResourceLink platform.
Can service providers register their services on ResourceLink?
Yes, providers can register through the "Contact Us" page to be verified and added to our platform.
Is there a mobile app available?
We are currently working on a mobile app to make accessing resources even easier. Stay tuned!
How do I reset my password?
If you have an account, you can reset your password from the login page using the "Forgot Password" link.
What areas does ResourceLink currently cover?
We currently serve major metro areas and are expanding. Check back often as new regions are added!
Can organizations update their own information?
Yes! Registered organizations have dashboard access or navigate to their settings page on the top right to updates to their listings.
What types of resources can I find here?
You can find food assistance, shelter, childcare, job support, legal aid, healthcare, and more.
How do I report incorrect or outdated information?
If you find outdated listings or incorrect information, please report them via the feedback form available on the Contact Us page.
Can I share a resource with someone else?
Yes! Please feel free to share to those who needs our platform
